Bio

My journey with digital art started in my sophomore year in highschool when I enrolled into a 3-year program called MCA; this program was highly focused around digital art. During these three years I would practice using features on applictions such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Premiere, & Adobe After Effects. The person application that I love to use the most out them all is Adobe Illustrator. Although I chose to major in marketing when I came to SJSU and I am now in my junior year, my love for digital art has not faded.

Through out the three years at SJSU, although I love marketing, I have been having an undying urge to get back into digital art. This is why this semester I decided to enroll into Intro to Digital Media and Intro to Digital Video.
